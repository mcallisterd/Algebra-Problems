Groups

  1. Section 1: Definition of a group.
    Aluffi defines a group, gives a simple example or two, and develops some basic group properties.
    Notes Exercises
  2. Section 2: Examples of groups.
    We delve into more examples of groups, such as symmetric groups and cyclic groups, as well as dihedral groups.
    Notes Exercises
  3. Section 3: The category Grp.
    We explore the category Grp, whose objects are groups and arrows/morphisms are group homomorphisms. Products and coproducts (in Abelian groups) are developed.
    Notes Exercises
  4. Section 4: Group homomorphisms.
    We get a better sense for homomorphisms operationally in Groups, exploring many properties, examples, and applications.
